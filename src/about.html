About this Site

This site is about showing Intel's history and innovation over the past 40 years. It shows a timeline of some of their major accomplishments, such as when they first made the microprocessor. It goes by decade and describes some points on their accomplishments for that decade. There is also a page on some of their mainstream current products that most people use today. This page contains the specs of the hardware and a little information of what each specification is. The main belly of this website is meant to be the timeline though, because Intel could have built the first PC but didn't, we'd like to show you the other accomplishments they have achieved instead, essentially without their microprocessor the Personal Computer could not have been invented.
